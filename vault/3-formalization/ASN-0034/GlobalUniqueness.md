**GlobalUniqueness (GlobalUniqueness).** No two distinct allocations, anywhere in the system, at any time, produce the same address.

*Proof.* We must show that for any two addresses `a` and `b` produced by distinct allocation events вЂ” whether by the same allocator, different allocators at the same level, or allocators at different levels of the hierarchy вЂ” `a в‰  b`. *Case 1: Same producing allocator.* Both `a` and `b` belong to `dom(A)` for the same allocator `A`.
