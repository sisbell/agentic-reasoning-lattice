# Cone Review — ASN-0034/TA-assoc (cycle 5)

*2026-04-17 23:14*

### TA-assoc *Lengths* paragraph applies TA0 to `a ⊕ (b ⊕ c)` before its preconditions are discharged
**Foundation**: TA0 (WellDefinedAddition, this ASN) — postcondition `#(a ⊕ w) = #w` is delivered only under preconditions `Pos(w)` and `actionPoint(w) ≤ #a`; TA-Pos and ActionPoint, the sister properties supplying those preconditions for `w = b ⊕ c`.
**ASN**: TA-assoc (AdditionAssociative), *Lengths* paragraph: "For the right side, `#(b ⊕ c) = #c`, **and `#(a ⊕ (b ⊕ c)) = #(b ⊕ c) = #c`**. Both sides have length `#c`." This paragraph appears before the *Action point of `s`* paragraph (which discharges `Pos(s)` and computes `actionPoint(s) = min(k_b, k_c)`) and before the *Domain conditions* paragraph (which establishes `min(k_b, k_c) ≤ #a` from `k_b ≤ #a`).
**Issue**: Applying TA0's result-length identity to `a ⊕ s` (where `s = b ⊕ c`) requires `Pos(s)` and `actionPoint(s) ≤ #a` as preconditions. At the point in *Lengths* where `#(a ⊕ (b ⊕ c)) = #(b ⊕ c)` is asserted, neither precondition has been discharged: `Pos(s)` is established only later in *Action point of `s`* (via the nonzero component at `min(k_b, k_c)` witnessing TA-Pos's existential), and `actionPoint(s) ≤ #a` requires both that result and *Domain conditions*' subsumption argument (`k_b ≤ #a ⟹ min(k_b, k_c) ≤ #a`). The structurally identical step on the left side (`#((a ⊕ b) ⊕ c) = #c`) is fine because its preconditions `Pos(c)` and `k_c ≤ #(a ⊕ b) = #b` are already in the formal-contract preconditions; but the right-side step's preconditions are intermediate conclusions established later in the proof. Per-step citation discipline elsewhere in this ASN (TumblerAdd's dominance proof, TA0's own proof) is careful to discharge each precondition before consuming the matching postcondition.
**What needs resolving**: Either reorder the proof so *Action point of `s`* (establishing `Pos(s)`) and the right-side portion of *Domain conditions* (establishing `actionPoint(s) ≤ #a`) precede the right-side length computation, or split *Lengths* into a left-side computation early and a right-side computation deferred until after the prerequisite discharges, so that every TA0 invocation is licensed at the point it occurs.
