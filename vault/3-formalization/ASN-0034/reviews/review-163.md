# Cone Review — ASN-0034/TA-Pos (cycle 2)

*2026-04-17 14:03*

### Six of the nine NAT-* axioms named in T0's "exhaustive list" are never stated as formal contracts in the ASN
**Foundation**: T0 (CarrierSetDefinition) — T0's prose claims nine NAT-* axioms "are stated as separate axioms": NAT-closure, NAT-order, NAT-discrete, NAT-addcompat, NAT-wellorder, NAT-zero, NAT-sub, NAT-cancel, NAT-addassoc, and adds "This list is exhaustive: the ASN states no NAT-* axiom outside it."
**ASN**: Only three NAT-* sections with *Formal Contract: Axiom:* blocks appear in the document — **NAT-order (NatStrictTotalOrder)**, **NAT-discrete (NatDiscreteness)**, and **NAT-wellorder (NatWellOrdering)**. NAT-closure, NAT-addcompat, NAT-zero, NAT-sub, NAT-cancel, and NAT-addassoc are not given standalone sections or Axiom blocks anywhere in the ASN. T1's *Depends* cites NAT-addcompat at ten sites and NAT-cancel at one (sub-case (ii, ii) "right cancellation at the fixed summand `1`"); TA-Pos's *Depends* cites NAT-zero ("NAT-zero's axiom `(A n ∈ ℕ :: 0 ≤ n)` discharges exactly this lower-bound premise"). These three axioms are load-bearing for two different proofs but have no Formal Contract in scope to be cited.
**Issue**: The exhaustive-list claim in T0 is a standing invariant the rest of the ASN relies on — downstream *Depends* lines are phrased as if each NAT-* name resolves to a formally stated axiom with a specific signature. Six of the nine names resolve to nothing. This leaves every NAT-addcompat, NAT-cancel, and NAT-zero citation in T1 and TA-Pos grounded only in T0's *description* of the axiom's content rather than in a stated axiom, and makes the exhaustive-list claim itself false as stated ("the ASN states no NAT-* axiom outside it" presupposes that the nine are stated inside it). NAT-sub, NAT-addassoc, and NAT-closure are named but never cited — either they are dead entries in T0's list or they will be cited by sections not included in this ASN; either way, the list and the citations are not reconciled.
**What needs resolving**: Either (a) give each of the six missing names its own formal-contract section with an *Axiom:* block stating the precise content (e.g., NAT-zero's `(A n ∈ ℕ :: 0 ≤ n)`, NAT-addcompat's strict successor inequality, NAT-cancel's right-cancellation clause at a fixed summand) so that downstream *Depends* citations resolve, or (b) narrow T0's list to only the axioms actually stated and actually cited, and amend the *Depends* lines that currently invoke absent axioms to cite only what is in scope, or (c) if these six are intended to live in a sibling foundation ASN rather than this one, convert T0's claim from "stated as separate axioms" (this ASN) to a *Depends* on that sibling, and treat the NAT-* names here as foundation imports rather than internal axioms.
