### TumblerAdd's strict-advancement and dominance proofs invoke ℕ additive properties not enumerated in T0
**Foundation**: `T0 (CarrierSetDefinition)` — currently enumerates "closure under successor and addition", strict-total-order properties of `<` on ℕ (irreflexivity, trichotomy, transitivity), the `≤` definition, and discreteness.
**ASN**: `TumblerAdd`, strict-advancement proof: "Since `k` is the first nonzero component of `w`, we have `wₖ ≥ 1`, so `rₖ = aₖ + wₖ ≥ aₖ + 1 > aₖ`." `TumblerAdd`, dominance proof equality sub-case: "when `aₖ = 0`, `rₖ = wₖ`" (silently using `0 + wₖ = wₖ`). `TumblerAdd`'s Depends T0 entry reads only: "membership `a ⊕ w ∈ T` is concluded via T0's characterisation of T as finite sequences over ℕ with length ≥ 1."
**Issue**: The strict-advancement chain `rₖ = aₖ + wₖ ≥ aₖ + 1 > aₖ` rests on two ℕ facts not enumerated in T0: (i) order-compatibility of addition (`wₖ ≥ 1 ⟹ aₖ + wₖ ≥ aₖ + 1`); and (ii) `n < n + 1` for `n ∈ ℕ` (to conclude `aₖ + 1 > aₖ`). The dominance proof additionally depends on (iii) `0` being a left-identity for addition (`0 + wₖ = wₖ`). T0 enumerates closure under addition and the ordering relations of `<`, but not these algebraic facts. Cycle 4 widened T0 to enumerate the strict-total-order properties of `<` that downstream proofs rely on; the analogous additive structure (additive identity, monotonicity of `+`, strict successor inequality) was not addressed. Even if T0 supplied these, `TumblerAdd`'s Depends T0 entry cites T0 only for membership in T, not for the additive reasoning that the strict-advancement and dominance proofs invoke. A formalizer building the proof-obligation set from Depends has no source in scope for the chain `... ≥ aₖ + 1 > aₖ` or the identity step `0 + wₖ = wₖ`.
**What needs resolving**: Either (i) extend T0's `Axiom` field to enumerate the additive ℕ properties downstream proofs rely on (additive identity, order-compatibility of `+`, and `n < n + 1`), and widen `TumblerAdd`'s Depends T0 entry to cite T0 for these facts at the strict-advancement and dominance proof sites; or (ii) introduce a named background axiom for ℕ's ordered-semiring structure that `TumblerAdd` and any future arithmetic property cites explicitly.

### ActionPoint's third postcondition invokes "nonzero natural ⇒ ≥ 1" without citing T0's discreteness axiom
**Foundation**: `T0 (CarrierSetDefinition)` — discreteness axiom `m ≤ n < m + 1 ⟹ n = m` is what licenses "nonzero natural ⇒ `≥ 1`" (no `m ∈ ℕ` with `0 < m < 1`).
**ASN**: `ActionPoint`, postconditions: "`1 ≤ actionPoint(w) ≤ #w`; `wᵢ = 0` for all `i < actionPoint(w)`; `w_{actionPoint(w)} ≥ 1`". Body: "From this definition, ... `w_{actionPoint(w)} ≥ 1`. ∎". Depends T0 entry: "the precondition `w ∈ T`, the length `#w`, and the component projection `wᵢ` used in the definition and postconditions all come from T0's characterisation of T as finite sequences over ℕ with length ≥ 1."
**Issue**: The third postcondition `w_{actionPoint(w)} ≥ 1` follows from `w_{actionPoint(w)} ≠ 0` (by definition of the action point as the least index where `wᵢ ≠ 0`) together with the ℕ inference "nonzero ⇒ `≥ 1`", which is licensed by T0's discreteness. Cycle 5 widened `PositiveTumbler`'s Depends T0 entry to call out exactly this inference for the analogous step `tₖ ≥ 1 as a nonzero natural number`. `ActionPoint` uses the same inference for its third postcondition but its Depends T0 entry cites only carrier set, length, and component projection — discreteness is not named, even though the postcondition cannot be discharged from carrier/length/projection alone. A formalizer scanning Depends entries to license the postconditions has no source for the strict inequality.
**What needs resolving**: `ActionPoint`'s Depends T0 entry should be widened to record T0's discreteness axiom as the licensing source for the third postcondition's "nonzero natural ⇒ `≥ 1`" step, matching the convention now used by `PositiveTumbler`.
