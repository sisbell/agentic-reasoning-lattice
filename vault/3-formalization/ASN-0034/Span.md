## Definition (Span)

**Definition (Span).** A *span* is a pair `(s, ‚Ąď)` where `s ‚ąą T` is a start address and `‚Ąď ‚ąą T` is a length ‚ÄĒ a positive tumbler used as a displacement whose action point satisfies `actionPoint(‚Ąď) ‚Č§ #s` ‚ÄĒ denoting the contiguous range from `s` up to but not including `s ‚äē ‚Ąď`. The four conditions `s ‚ąą T`, `‚Ąď ‚ąą T`, `Pos(‚Ąď)`, and `actionPoint(‚Ąď) ‚Č§ #s` are precisely TA0's preconditions for `s ‚äē ‚Ąď ‚ąą T` under the instantiation `(a, w) := (s, ‚Ąď)`, so under them the upper bound denotes a tumbler in `T` and the set `{t ‚ąą T : s ‚Č§ t < s ‚äē ‚Ąď}` ‚ÄĒ the span ‚ÄĒ is well-defined; pairs `(s, ‚Ąď)` violating any of them are not spans.

- *Preconditions:* `s ‚ąą T`, `‚Ąď ‚ąą T`, `Pos(‚Ąď)`, `actionPoint(‚Ąď) ‚Č§ #s`
- *Definition:* `span(s, ‚Ąď) = {t ‚ąą T : s ‚Č§ t < s ‚äē ‚Ąď}`
- *Depends:* T0 (CarrierSetDefinition) ‚ÄĒ supplies the carrier `T` and the length operator `#`: `‚ąą T` appears at `s ‚ąą T` and `‚Ąď ‚ąą T` in the pair components and at `{t ‚ąą T : s ‚Č§ t < s ‚äē ‚Ąď}` in the defining set, and `#s` appears in the precondition `actionPoint(‚Ąď) ‚Č§ #s`.
