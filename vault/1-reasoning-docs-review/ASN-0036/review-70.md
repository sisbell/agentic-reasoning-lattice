## OUT_OF_SCOPE

### Topic 1: Subtraction homomorphism conditions
**Why out of scope**: The ASN correctly identifies "Under what conditions on w does the subtraction homomorphism ord(v ⊖ w) = ord(v) ⊖ w_ord hold" as an open question. This requires careful analysis of TA7a's conditional S-membership results for subtraction and is future work, not an error in the current ASN.

### Topic 2: Maximal run decomposition uniqueness
**Why out of scope**: Whether the span decomposition has a unique maximal form (fewest runs) is listed as an open question. S8 establishes existence of some finite partition; uniqueness of the coarsest partition is a refinement for a future ASN.

### Topic 3: Operation-level D-CTG preservation
**Why out of scope**: Whether INSERT, DELETE, COPY, and REARRANGE each preserve D-CTG and D-MIN is explicitly noted as a "verification obligation for each operation's ASN" and listed in the scope exclusions.

VERDICT: CONVERGED
