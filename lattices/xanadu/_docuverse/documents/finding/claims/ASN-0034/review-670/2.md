### Divergence: postcondition specifies case (ii) values but not case (i)
**Class**: OBSERVE
**Foundation**: Divergence (Divergence)
**ASN**: Formal Contract, Postcondition bullets
**Issue**: The postcondition explicitly states the case (ii) values (`divergence(a, b) = #a + 1` in sub-case (ii-a), `= #b + 1` in sub-case (ii-b)) but gives only `divergence(a, b) ∈ ℕ` for case (i) — the least-k characterization with its minimality predicate appears only in the Definition bullet. A downstream claim that needs to reason about the case (i) value in terms of which components agree must look past the postcondition into the Definition.
