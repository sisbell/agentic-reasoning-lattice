**ASN**: GlobalUniqueness (GlobalUniqueness) — Formal Contract *Depends:* section; proof body, Case 5, k'₁ > k'₂ sub-case: "Rewriting `2 = 1 + 1` and applying NAT-addassoc gives `(#p₁ + 1) + 1 = #p₂ + 1`; NAT-cancel yields `#p₂ = #p₁ + 1`."
