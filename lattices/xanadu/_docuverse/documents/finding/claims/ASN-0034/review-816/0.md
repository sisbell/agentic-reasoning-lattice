### NAT-discrete missing NAT-closure in Depends

**What needs resolving**: NAT-closure must appear in NAT-discrete's Depends, annotated to supply the binary operation `+` and the element `1 ∈ ℕ` appearing in the axiom's consequent `m + 1 ≤ n` and in the Consequence's hypothesis `n < m + 1`.
