### T4 Depends annotation for T0 attributes the wrong carrier
**Class**: REVISE
**Foundation**: T0 (CarrierSetDefinition)
**ASN**: T4 (HierarchicalParsing) — *Depends:* entry for T0
**Issue**: The annotation reads "T0 (CarrierSetDefinition) — supplies the carrier ℕ, the tumbler length `#·`, and the index domain `{1, …, #t}`." T0 does not supply ℕ — that is NAT-carrier's role. T0 supplies T (the tumbler carrier), the length operator `#·`, the component-projection signature, and the index domain. Saying T0 supplies ℕ misattributes the source of ℕ and leaves the tumbler carrier T, the actual primary contribution of T0, unnamed. A downstream consumer tracing where the `t ∈ T` precondition gets its T from would look in the annotation and find ℕ instead.
**What needs resolving**: The T0 annotation must correctly name T (the tumbler carrier) as what T0 supplies. The claim that T0 supplies ℕ must be removed; ℕ is supplied by NAT-carrier, not by T0.
