# Review of ASN-0042

## REVISE

(none)

## OUT_OF_SCOPE

### Topic 1: Ownership transfer mechanism
**Why out of scope**: The ASN correctly records this as an open question. Transfer would require machinery external to the address-derived ownership model (a "deed" separate from the structural "birth certificate"). A future ASN would need to specify what invariants transfer preserves given that structural provenance (O6) is inalienable. This is new territory, not an error in this ASN.

### Topic 2: Cross-node identity federation
**Why out of scope**: O9 establishes that ownership is node-local. The ASN correctly identifies federation as an open question. Specifying what invariants a federated identity model must satisfy to remain consistent with O9 belongs in a future ASN.

VERDICT: CONVERGED
