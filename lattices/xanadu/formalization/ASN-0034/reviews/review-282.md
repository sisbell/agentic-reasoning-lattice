### TumblerSub's motivation uses NAT-addcompat's strict successor inequality and NAT-order's transitivity at the prefix-obstruction chain `zpd(b, a) ≥ #a + 1 > #a`, without citing either in Depends

**Foundation**: NAT-addcompat (NatAdditionOrderAndSuccessor) bundles the strict successor inequality `n < n + 1`. NAT-order (NatStrictTotalOrder) includes transitivity `m < n ∧ n < p ⟹ m < p`. T1's proof enumerates per-site counts of both axioms whenever the strict successor inequality or the composition `x ≥ y + 1 > y ⟹ x > y` appears (e.g., T1 part (a) Case (ii) cites NAT-addcompat for `m < m + 1`; T1 part (b) Case 3 cites both NAT-addcompat and NAT-order-transitivity where the strict successor composes with `n + 1 ≤ m`). TumblerSub's Depends already follows per-step accounting for T3, TumblerAdd, NAT-zero, NAT-order, and NAT-sub.

**ASN**: TumblerSub, motivation paragraph. "…when `a` is a proper sequence-prefix of `b`, the action point of `b ⊖ a` satisfies `actionPoint(b ⊖ a) = zpd(b, a) ≥ #a + 1 > #a`, violating TumblerAdd's precondition `actionPoint(w) ≤ #a`." Depends list: T0, T1, T3, Divergence, ZPD, TA-Pos, ActionPoint, TumblerAdd, NAT-sub, NAT-zero, NAT-order — no NAT-addcompat, and NAT-order's entry enumerates only trichotomy and defining-clause uses (no transitivity site).

**Issue**: The inequality chain `zpd(b, a) ≥ #a + 1 > #a` decomposes into two formal steps: (a) `#a + 1 > #a`, which is exactly NAT-addcompat's strict successor inequality `n < n + 1` instantiated at `n = #a`; and (b) composing `zpd(b, a) ≥ #a + 1` with `#a + 1 > #a` to conclude `zpd(b, a) > #a`, which is NAT-order's transitivity (in the strict disjunct of the unpacked `≥`, or substitution in the equality disjunct). Without these, the step `... ≥ #a + 1 > #a` violates TumblerAdd's precondition `actionPoint(w) ≤ #a` is not discharged — the chain merely asserts the composition. TumblerSub's Depends names NAT-order for trichotomy and ≤/< unfolding but does not enumerate transitivity, and NAT-addcompat is absent entirely. The motivation's TumblerAdd citation only supplies the threshold `≤ #a`; it does not supply `zpd(b, a) > #a`.

**What needs resolving**: TumblerSub must either (a) add NAT-addcompat to its Depends with per-site accounting at the motivational prefix-obstruction step, and extend NAT-order's entry to include a transitivity site for the `≥ #a + 1 > #a` composition, mirroring the per-site discipline already applied to T3 (ii), TumblerAdd (i)/(ii), and the other NAT-order roles; or (b) rewrite the motivation to avoid making this as a formal derivation (stating only that `zpd(b, a)` falls beyond `#a` without the `#a + 1` intermediate), so the per-step convention does not apply to the narrative; or (c) declare a global motivation-exemption convention and reconcile it with the per-site citations TumblerSub already supplies for T3 and TumblerAdd at motivational sites.
