# Cone Review — ASN-0034/TS5 (cycle 4)

*2026-04-18 14:20*

### TS3 and TS4 Depends omit TA-Pos and ActionPoint despite consuming `Pos(·)` and `actionPoint(·)` directly
**Foundation**: OrdinalShift's own Depends articulates the per-step citation discipline for these two symbols: "TA-Pos (PositiveTumbler) — invoked at TA0's third precondition check to supply the predicate `Pos(·)` that the check consumes... TA-Pos must be listed here rather than left to flow transitively through OrdinalDisplacement (whose postcondition only fixes the truth of `Pos(δ(n, m))`, not the defining meaning of the predicate)"; and the parallel "ActionPoint (ActionPoint) — introduces the operator `actionPoint(·)`... ActionPoint must be listed here rather than left to flow transitively through OrdinalDisplacement". OrdinalShift's Depends explicitly names TS1 as following the same discipline at its analogous consumption sites.
**ASN**: TS3's proof consumes both symbols at its second-shift TA0 precondition discharge: "`δ(n₁, m) ∈ T` and `Pos(δ(n₁, m))` transfer directly from OrdinalDisplacement's exported postconditions... and `actionPoint(δ(n₁, m)) ≤ #v` is exactly the `k = m ≤ m = #v` bound discharged at the first shift via OrdinalDisplacement's exported `actionPoint(δ(n₁, m)) = m`". TS3's Depends lists OrdinalShift, OrdinalDisplacement, T0, NAT-closure, NAT-addcompat, NAT-order, TA0, TumblerAdd, NAT-addassoc, T3 — but neither TA-Pos nor ActionPoint. Similarly, TS4's proof consumes both symbols at its TA-strict precondition checks ("*First precondition: Pos(δ(n, m))*" and "*Second precondition: actionPoint(δ(n, m)) ≤ #v*"), but TS4's Depends lists only OrdinalShift, OrdinalDisplacement, TA-strict, T0 — no TA-Pos, no ActionPoint.
**Issue**: Both TS3 and TS4 consume `Pos(·)` and `actionPoint(·)` as symbol-introducing citations (not only as postcondition values) but route them through OrdinalDisplacement's exported postconditions alone. Under the per-step discipline OrdinalShift itself articulates and TS1 follows, the defining meaning of these symbols is sourced to TA-Pos and ActionPoint respectively, which must be listed directly in Depends so that a reviser tightening either definition has visibility into TS3's and TS4's consumption sites. The omission breaks that visibility.
**What needs resolving**: TS3 and TS4 should each add TA-Pos and ActionPoint to their Depends lists (citing the sites at which `Pos(·)` and `actionPoint(·)` are consumed), or articulate the convention by which these two proofs diverge from the discipline OrdinalShift and TS1 follow.
