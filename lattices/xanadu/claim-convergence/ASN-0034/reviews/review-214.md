# Cone Review — ASN-0034/TA-strict (cycle 2)

*2026-04-18 00:09*

### TA0 duplicates TumblerAdd postconditions with the same redundancy pattern previously flagged for TA-strict
**Foundation**: TumblerAdd (this ASN) — Postconditions: "**a ⊕ w ∈ T**, **#(a ⊕ w) = #w**, a ⊕ w > a (T1), a ⊕ w ≥ w (T1, T3)"
**ASN**: TA0 (WellDefinedAddition) — Postconditions: "a ⊕ w ∈ T, #(a ⊕ w) = #w"; proof: "By TumblerAdd (this ASN), each component of the result lies in ℕ and `#(a ⊕ w) = #w ≥ 1`, so `a ⊕ w ∈ T`."
**Issue**: TA0's postconditions are verbatim the first two of TumblerAdd's four postconditions, and TA0's proof delegates entirely to TumblerAdd. This is the structural twin of the TA-strict redundancy flagged in Previous Findings — TA0 stands to TumblerAdd's first two postconditions as TA-strict stands to its third. The motivational paragraph ("The precondition `actionPoint(w) ≤ #a` is essential…") further conflates roles: it argues why the precondition matters *for TumblerAdd's construction*, which is an argument about TumblerAdd, not an independent content for TA0. Downstream consumers then route through a cascade (e.g., TA-strict cites TA0 to discharge `a ⊕ w ∈ T`, but TumblerAdd already exports exactly that claim) — so TA0 sits as an opaque relabelling layer between sister properties that could cite TumblerAdd directly. A reviser tightening TumblerAdd's membership argument or the result-length identity has two "entry points" to keep aligned with no documented relationship between them; the ASN does not explain why TA0 exists as a distinct property if TumblerAdd already exports the same claims.
**What needs resolving**: TA0 must either (a) be eliminated in favor of callers citing TumblerAdd's postconditions directly (parallel to what was contemplated for TA-strict), or (b) state explicitly why TA0 exists as a distinct labelled export (e.g., as the canonical citation handle for downstream ASNs) and — if retained — make its relationship to TumblerAdd's postcondition list explicit, just as the revised TA-strict does in its motivational paragraph. The current cascade TA-strict → TA0 → TumblerAdd for membership must also be either justified or collapsed to a direct citation.
