# Sub-Questions — Heat and the Constitution of Matter

**Inquiry:** How is heat connected to the constitution of matter?

1. [theory] What does the theory identify as the microscopic substrate of heat within matter?
2. [theory] How does the internal motion of constituent particles give rise to the thermal state of a body?
3. [theory] What role do translational, rotational, and vibrational degrees of freedom play in storing thermal energy?
4. [theory] How does the theory partition energy among the internal modes of a polyatomic constituent?
5. [theory] What connection does the theory draw between temperature and the mean kinetic energy of constituent motion?
6. [theory] How do intermolecular forces between constituents modify the purely kinetic account of heat?
7. [theory] What does the theory predict about the heat capacity of a body from the number of active degrees of freedom of its constituents?
8. [theory] How does the theory account for the distinction between sensible heat and latent heat in terms of constituent arrangement?
9. [theory] What does the theory say about the transfer of thermal energy through collisions between constituents?
10. [theory] How does the structural phase of matter — its degree of internal order or disorder — constrain the distribution of thermal energy among its constituents?
11. [evidence] What specific heat values are reported for solid elemental substances in the corpus, and at what temperatures were they measured?
12. [evidence] For which elements are molar heat capacities (specific heat × atomic weight) reported, and what numerical values do they take?
13. [evidence] Do any substances in the data show a roughly constant product of specific heat and atomic weight across different elements?
14. [evidence] What measurement methods (calorimetry, method of mixtures, cooling curves) are cited, and on which substances were they applied?
15. [evidence] Across what temperature range were the heat-capacity measurements performed, and do any substances show temperature-dependent specific heats?
16. [evidence] Which compounds (as distinct from pure elements) appear in the corpus with measured heat capacities, and what values are reported?
17. [evidence] Are there reported measurements of latent heat for phase changes (melting, vaporization) in any substances, and what values?
18. [evidence] What atomic weights are assigned to the elements in the corpus, and from which source or convention?
19. [evidence] Do any substances in the data deviate from otherwise regular heat-capacity patterns, and what are the outliers?
20. [evidence] Are heat capacities reported for substances in different physical states (solid vs. liquid vs. gas) for the same substance?
