## Question 17 [evidence]

> Are there reported measurements of latent heat for phase changes (melting, vaporization) in any substances, and what values?

The corpus contains no reported measurements of latent heat for phase changes (melting or vaporization) in any substance.

The 1819 Dulong-Petit paper [1819-dulong-petit] focuses exclusively on specific heats of solid elements and their relation to atomic weights. Latent heat is mentioned conceptually — in critiquing Lavoisier's theory that heat released during combustion derives from changes of state — but no numerical values for latent heats are given anywhere in the text.
