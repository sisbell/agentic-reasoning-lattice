## Question 15 [evidence]

> Across what temperature range were the heat-capacity measurements performed, and do any substances show temperature-dependent specific heats?

**Temperature range:** All measurements were performed within an interval of **5° to 10° centigrade above the ambient medium** [1819-dulong-petit]. The ambient medium was held at 0°C by surrounding the apparatus with melting ice, so the effective measurement range was approximately **5°C to 10°C**.

**Temperature-dependent specific heats:** The corpus contains no such data. Dulong and Petit conducted measurements only within that narrow band, and no substance is reported as showing variation across it. The paper does not address temperature dependence at all — it reports single specific-heat values per substance.
